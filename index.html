Introduction to the Course

Probability and Statistics form the mathematical foundation for analyzing data and dealing with uncertainty. This course introduces systematic methods to collect, summarize, and interpret data using descriptive statistical measures and visual tools.

The course also covers statistical inference techniques such as estimation and hypothesis testing, which help in making reliable and informed decisions based on data analysis.
