Discrete & Continuous Variables

A random variable is a numerical quantity whose value depends on the outcome of a random experiment. Random variables are used to quantify uncertainty in probabilistic and statistical analysis. Random variables are broadly classified into: Discrete Random Variables Continuous Random VariablesDiscrete variables have countable values; continuous have infinite values.
