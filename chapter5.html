Python-based Data Analysis

Python is one of the most widely used programming languages for data analysis, probability, and statistical computing. Its simplicity, readability, and powerful libraries make it ideal for analyzing data, visualizing results, and performing statistical computations. In Probability and Statistics, Python helps automate calculations, analyze large datasets, and visualize distributions such as binomial, Poisson, and normal distributions.

Data Cleaning & Visualization

Remove errors and visualize data using graphs for insights. In Probability and Statistics, raw data collected from real-world sources is often incomplete, inconsistent, or noisy. Data cleaning ensures data quality, while data visualization helps in understanding distributions, trends, and patterns. Python provides powerful libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib to perform efficient data cleaning and visualization.

Histogram & Boxplot Generation

Used to understand data distributions visually. Graphical representation of data is essential in Probability and Statistics to understand distribution, spread, and variability of data. Two important graphical tools are the Histogram and the Box Plot.
